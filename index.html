About

A senior designer for ambitious business visuals

Sak Graphics is a creative design brand based in Muscat, Oman, focused on professional visual design for businesses, brands, campaigns, and individuals.

Led by Sajid Ali Khan, Sak Graphics brings senior-level graphic design thinking to branding, logo design, social media creatives, business cards, posters, brochures, banners, packaging visuals, and marketing materials. The brand is positioned for businesses that want confident, clean, and creative designs, and Sajid is ready to take up challenging design work across different industries.
